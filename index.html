ABOUT US

Hackerearth Hub NMAMIT is a club started in May 2021. This club is backed by Hackerearth and it ensures the enrichment of competitive coding skills and works with the aim of imparting a good competitive coding habit among the peers.

OBJECTIVES

  • The club was started with the sole aim of familiarizing and promoting competitive coding among student
  • We host webinars on different topics after receiving the feedback from our peers
  • We promote our club activities through different social media platform
  • We host a weekly online contest, where questions are framed according to difficulty level as it can be solved by the students
